About the game:

A classic game of concentration. To start, click a card to flip and reveal it. Try to find a matching card by flipping another card. If the second card is not a match, both cards will flip back to their original covered positions. If both cards are the same, you have successfully made one matching pair. Complete all nine matching pairs to win the game.
